# PlacePrep – Student Learning & Placement Preparation Platform

PlacePrep is a student-focused learning and placement-preparation platform designed to help learners succeed in aptitude tests, technical rounds, and job interviews.

The platform provides detailed company insights such as required CGPA/SGPA, recommended preparation levels, and past hiring patterns. Students can also track their learning progress through an integrated calendar and analytics dashboard.

PlacePrep combines structured learning resources, intelligent preparation tools, and real-time progress tracking to create a unified ecosystem for placement preparation.

---

# Core AI Features

### AI Mock Interviews

Mock interviews are dynamically generated based on the student’s preparation progress, helping them practice questions tailored to their skill level and target companies.

### AI Resume Builder

The AI Resume Builder automatically pulls data from:

* GitHub
* LinkedIn
* Student activity within the platform

Every time the user clicks **“Download Resume”**, the system generates a customized resume optimized for the selected company.

# Why Many Students Fail Interviews

Several studies and industry reports highlight major gaps in placement preparation.

1. According to the **Unstop Talent Report 2024**, **66% of recruiters say skill gaps are a major challenge in campus hiring**.

2. **Mettl**, an assessment platform, reports that many students fail during **aptitude tests and reasoning assessments**, which are commonly used during placements.

3. Research on student employability indicates that many students struggle during:

   * Written assessments
   * Performance tests
   * Group discussions
   * One-on-one interviews

4. Academic research shows that **80% of faculty members use aptitude tests** to evaluate student readiness for employment.

5. This indicates that aptitude tests are one of the most critical barriers in campus hiring.

6. According to a **Times of India report**, **only 1 in 3 job seekers feel interview-ready**.
